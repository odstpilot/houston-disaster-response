Important Notes During Disasters:

  • Hospital availability can change during severe weather events
  • Call ahead to confirm services and accessibility
  • Monitor local emergency alerts for hospital status updates
  • Emergency departments may have extended wait times during disasters
  • Bring identification, insurance cards, and current medications if possible
  • For non-urgent care, consider urgent care centers or telehealth options
